About Me

I am a Software Development Diploma Graduate from Bow Valley College in Calgary, AB. My passion in software development is in web development, particularly with the React framework.

I have had great success in my previous career as a Seismic Data Technologist in the Oil & Gas Industry. I worked with big data, machine language, database management and algorithmic workflows. The transition to Software Development was natural.

I have also worked in sound design and composition while volunteering at Loose Moose Theatre. I worked there as an operator / improvisor / designer. My passion with music steered me towards a side profession of DJaying.
